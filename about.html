• about me

Hello, my name is Angela, and welcome to my website. Let me tell you a bit about myself.

I was born in Glasgow, Scotland, but spent my early childhood in Oxfordshire, England, and Hefei, China. At age 5, I moved with my family to The Woodlands, a surburb north of Houston, TX. As a kid, I enjoyed drawing, reading, and going on the internet. I started using the internet at a rather young age, and I first learned HTML while trying to edit my Neopets webpage.

As a giant nerd in high school, I took a lot of art and art history classes but also the two computer science classes my school offered and was a lab aide for computer science my senior year. I started college at The University of Texas at Austin in August 2014, majoring in Plan II Honors and Computer Science (Turing Scholars Honors). The summer after my freshman year, I had an internship at IBM working on Bluemix Guided Experience.

Currently, I am in my second year, still at UT Austin. I'll be interning at Google next summer and most likely completing a thesis in computer art next school year. At this time, I am trying to complete a 365-day streak on Github. Follow me here.
